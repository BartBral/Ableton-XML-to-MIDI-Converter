Ableton XML to MIDI Converter

Select an Ableton project folder or any directory with .alc, .agr, .xml, .als, or .adv files. The tool scans subfolders, extracts MIDI data, converts it to .mid, and saves everything as a zipped folder in your Downloads. Ableton files are auto-decompressed if needed. Files without MIDI notes are skipped, and errors are shown in the console.
Note: .alc, .agr give best results, and .als files may yield poor results. No files ever leave your computer, nothing gets uploaded anywhere.
